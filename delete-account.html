Delete Your Account & Data

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Tally account and the data associated with it. Tally is a personal balance tracker built by an independent developer.

Request full account & data deletion

To delete your Tally account and all of your cloud-synced data, follow these steps:

  1. Email us from the email address linked to your Tally (Google) account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my account and data".
  3. Optionally include your display name so we can locate your records faster.
tallyapp@proton.me

We will confirm your request and complete the deletion within 30 days (usually much sooner).

Delete data yourself, in the app

You don't need to contact us to remove individual items or local data:

  • Delete or archive individual activities, projects, lending records, and transactions directly in the app (swipe to delete, or use the Remove option).
  • To remove all data stored on your device, sign out and clear your browser's site data / app storage.

What gets deleted

When you request full deletion, we permanently remove:

  • Your account record and the Google profile information we store (display name, email address).
  • All of your cloud-synced data in Google Firebase Firestore — activities, projects, balances, lending records, timers, categories, and settings.

What is kept, and retention period

After your request is processed, your data is deleted from our active database immediately and purged from backups within 30 days. We do not retain your personal data after deletion, except where we are required to by law.

Contact

Questions about deleting your account or data? Reach us at tallyapp@proton.me.
