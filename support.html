Support

Tally is a simple balance tracker — activities, group expenses, lending and project budgets, all in one place. This page answers the most common questions.

Do I need an account?

No. Enter a first name and start using Tally right away — everything is stored on your device. Signing in (Google, or Apple where available) is optional and adds backup and sync across devices.

How do I get my data on a new phone?

If you signed in, just sign in with the same account on the new device — your trackers appear automatically. If you never signed in, your data lives only on the old device: sign in there first, then sign in on the new phone.

What does Settle Up do?

It draws a line under the current balance and resets it to zero — your full history is kept, and you can undo the settlement for a few seconds right after tapping it.

Is my data private?

Yes. No ads, no analytics, no selling of data. Without an account nothing ever leaves your device; with an account your data is stored in Google Firebase, used only to provide sync. Full details in the Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account and data?

Follow the steps on the account deletion page.
